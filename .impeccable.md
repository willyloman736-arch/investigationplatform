# Digital Asset Investigations — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
People who have been scammed out of crypto and are filing a recovery complaint — often anxious, wary of being scammed again, and evaluating whether this platform is trustworthy in the first seconds. Plus invited operators and internal admins working cases. The auth/landing surfaces are a first-impression trust test; the dashboard/admin surfaces are working tools used repeatedly.

### Brand Personality
Trustworthy, secure, structured. Institutional credibility with a modern, precise edge — reassuring without being flashy or "salesy." It should feel like a regulated-adjacent financial/forensic tool, not a hype crypto product. Emotional goal: calm confidence.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, deep navy-black stage ("Cyber Blue + Ice": primary `#2563EB`, ice-cyan accents/focus `#67E8F9`) with **purposeful glassmorphism** (frosted cards, layered depth, inset highlights) — already the established design language. Premium = depth, restraint, and craft (layered shadows, real elevation, refined spacing/typography, considered motion), not neon or decoration.
- **Theme:** dark (canonical; the whole app ships dark).
- **Type:** Inter app-wide (kept for consistency; hierarchy comes from weight/size/tracking, not font swaps).
- **Tokens:** HSL CSS variables in `app/globals.css` + glass utilities (`.glass`, `.glass-card`, `.glass-strong`, `.glow-ice`).
- **Anti-references:** neon "AI-slop" dashboards, gradient text, fake trust badges/unearned legitimacy signals, gimmicky bounce/elastic motion.

### Design Principles
1. **Honest by construction** — never imply certifications, partnerships, or fund movement that don't exist. Trust is earned through clarity, not borrowed logos.
2. **Depth over decoration** — glass and shadow convey real elevation and focus; accents (ice/blue) stay rare and meaningful (~10% weight).
3. **Calm, legible, accessible** — WCAG-AA contrast on glass, visible focus, 44px touch targets, `prefers-reduced-motion` respected; motion animates transform/opacity only.
4. **Consistency is the system** — align to existing tokens, glass utilities, font, and spacing scale; don't fork one-off styles.
5. **One memorable moment** — a single, well-orchestrated interaction (e.g. the auth card's subtle 3D tilt + entrance) beats scattered micro-effects.
